The Petersen-Estimator (and much more) for Two-Sample Capture-Recapture Studies with Applications to Fisheries Management

This is a monograph summarizing work on the Petersen estimator using conditional likelihood estimation with a related R package. Includes chapters on
  • Standard Petersen; covariates; model selection;
  • Stratified Petersen; fixed strata; geographical stratification (SPAS); temporal stratification (BTSPAS)
  • Incomplete stratification;
  • Double tagging to estimation and adjust for tag loss
  • Mulitple Marks; multiple Petersens
  • Forward and backward Petersens
  • Genetic capture-recapture
